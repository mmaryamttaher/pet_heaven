Login Issues

Forgot Password

1. Click on the "Forgot Password?" button.

2. Enter the email associated with your account.

3. Receive a password reset link in your email.

4. Click the link and set a new password.

Forgot Username

1. Click on the "Forgot Username?" button.

2. Enter the email associated with your account.

3. Receive an email containing your username.

Incorrect Password

1. Ensure your password is entered correctly (case-sensitive).

2. Use the "Show Password" option to verify.

3. If the issue persists, use the "Forgot Password?" option.

Technical Issues

1. Check your internet connection.

2. Try logging in using a different browser or device.

3. Clear your browser's cache and cookies.

4. If the problem persists, contact technical support.

Account Locked

1. Wait for the lockout period to expire (usually a few minutes to hours).

2. If your account is still locked, contact support to unlock it manually.

Other Issues

1. Verify that you are using the correct login credentials.

2. Check if your account has been deactivated or banned.

3. For other specific issues, contact the support team for assistance.
