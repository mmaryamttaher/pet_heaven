App Not Working

App Doesn't Open

1. Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.

2. Close the app and restart it.

3. Restart your device.

4. Make sure you have enough storage space on your device.

5. If the issue persists, try reinstalling the app.

App Crashes

1. Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.

2. Close other apps running in the background to free up resources.

3. Clear the app's cache through the device settings.

4. Reinstall the app if the issue continues.
