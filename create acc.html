How To Create An Account

  • Go to the website's homepage.
  • Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner of the page.
  • Fill in your personal details: name, email address, and a secure password.
  • Accept the terms and conditions by ticking the checkbox.
  • Click "Create Account."
  • Check your email for a verification link, and click on it to activate your account.
  • Once verified, log in with your email and password to access your account.
